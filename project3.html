Localization and Freelance Utilization at Trendhim

Introduction

The core focus of this project was to explore whether Trendhim can overcome its localization challenges in its international expansion and to investigate the role that freelancers play in this process.

Objectives

The primary objective of this project is to determine how Trendhim can effectively address localization challenges during its international expansion and to assess the impact of freelancers in this process. Specifically, the project aims to:

  • Identify Localization Challenges: Understand the barriers Trendhim faces in different international markets.
  • Evaluate Freelance Contributions: Assess how freelancers contribute to overcoming these localization challenges.
  • Enhance Localization Strategies: Develop strategies that integrate freelancers to improve Trendhim’s localization efforts.

Key Features

The project incorporates several key features and methodologies to achieve its objectives:

  • Localization Strategies: Examines Trendhim's approaches to language adaptation, cultural nuances, and local market strategies. Highlights how freelancers support these initiatives by providing localized expertise and flexibility.
  • Methodology for Empirical Knowledge Generation: Utilizes an abductive approach, involving in-depth interviews with three Trendhim employees and an exploratory interview. The Gioia method is used for coding and categorizing data into ‘1st Order Concepts’ and ‘2nd Order Themes,’ aligning empirical findings with theoretical insights.
  • Methodology for Theoretical Knowledge Generation: Conducts extensive literature reviews using sources from Summon, Google Scholar, and EBSCO - Business Source Complete. Focuses on localization, internationalization, and the role of freelancers. Synthesizes key theoretical frameworks to support empirical findings, providing a comprehensive understanding of freelancers' impact on localization strategies.
  • Key Findings:
    • Freelancers in the 21st Century: Highlights the evolving role of freelancers in modern economies and their impact on business operations.
    • Value and Satisfaction: Discusses the advantages and challenges associated with freelance work, including job satisfaction and quality.
    • E-commerce Localization: Provides insights into cultural adaptations and strategic adjustments necessary for global platforms, shifting from standardization to localization.

Conclusion

The study concluded that Trendhim’s localization strategies have significantly enhanced its market presence. The effective integration of freelancers is crucial for overcoming challenges in international expansion, as freelancers provide the necessary flexibility and localized expertise essential for adapting to diverse markets. Freelancers enable Trendhim to efficiently implement localization mechanisms such as language adaptation and cultural customization, thereby maintaining competitiveness and achieving a sustainable competitive advantage. The findings underscore the importance of a balanced approach that combines internal resources with freelance support to ensure successful localization and continued growth in international markets.
