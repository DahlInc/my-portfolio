Data-Driven Stock Market Analysis: Power BI Visualizations for Smarter Investment Decisions

Introduction

In this project, I utilized Power BI to perform in-depth financial analysis on stock market data, focusing on key metrics such as market capitalization, volatility, price momentum, and P/E ratios. The goal was to provide investors with a comprehensive view of market trends and company performance, enabling data-driven decisions for both short-term trading and long-term investments. By visualizing these metrics, the analysis offers clear insights into risk, timing, and valuation in the stock market

Objectives

The project aims to automate the extraction of up-to-date stock market data, perform comprehensive financial analyses, and visualize the results using Power BI. By streamlining data collection and analysis, MarketMaster empowers investors to make informed decisions for both short-term trading and long-term investments.

Key Features

MarketMaster includes: Market Cap Analysis: Categorizes companies into large, mid, and small-cap to assess market presence. Volatility Analysis: Evaluates stock risk by measuring daily price fluctuations. Price Momentum Analysis: Uses simple moving averages to identify and visualize price trends. P/E Ratio Comparison: Compares Price-to-Earnings ratios to assess stock valuations relative to peers.

Results & Purpose

This project provides a comprehensive view of the stock market, balancing risk, timing, and value. Automated data scraping ensures accuracy and timeliness, while Power BI visualizations translate complex data into actionable insights. This project equips investors with the tools needed to enhance portfolio diversification and optimize investment strategies through informed decision-making.
